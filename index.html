The Technology

How It Works

01
📸
Image Loading
Your image loads into HTML5 Canvas in-browser. Nothing ever leaves your device.
02
🔬
Pixel Sampling
We sample up to 50,000 pixels building a representative dataset of every color present.
03
🧠
K-Means++ Clustering
Our ML algorithm groups similar pixels into clusters, converging on the true dominant colors.
04
🎨
Color Conversion
Each cluster converts to HEX, RGB, HSL with coverage percentage and auto-generated color names.
